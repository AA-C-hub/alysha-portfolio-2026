Phoebe Judge's

Book Trilogy

  • Client: Student Project
  • Project Type: Intro to Graphic Design
  • Software: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, & InDesign

The Challenges

Through extensive exploration and sketching, I chose to focus on a single theme. I found that optical illusions best suited the overall atmosphere of the episodes I listened to. Each cover represents a different episode, capturing the essence of the narratives through visual elements and symbolism.

Vivid with meaning

The colors and graphics are intentional to each story to subtly hint at what the stories may reveal without giving away the plot. This ties the concept of optical illusions about how each story holds more than what meets the eye.
